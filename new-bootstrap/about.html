Who We Are

Established in **1990**, Charulekh Prakashan has built an enviable status for itself as a distinguished entity based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. We are proudly engaged as a **Manufacturer, Service Provider, Trader, and Wholesaler** of a comprehensive range of spiritual and astrological products and services.

Our journey began with a profound commitment to disseminate authentic spiritual knowledge and make ancient wisdom accessible to all. Over the decades, we have continuously evolved, leveraging modern technology to fulfill our mission. We are dedicated to providing guidance that illuminates the path to inner peace and prosperity.

Our extensive offerings include profound **Astrology Books, Numerology Books, and Tantra Books**, alongside **Havan Audio CDs** and **Mantra Collections**. We also provide a selection of high-quality **Natural Gemstones, Pure Agarbatti, and Hawan Samagri**. Furthermore, our **Pratyabhigyaan Magazine** offers monthly spiritual insights and horoscopes, complemented by comprehensive **Online Courses** in Astrology, Numerology, and various spiritual practices. For personalized guidance, we offer expert **Horoscope Readings, Gemstone Recommendations, and Spiritual Counseling.**

We strive to uphold the highest standards of quality and authenticity in all our products and services, ensuring that every offering from Charulekh Prakashan contributes positively to your spiritual journey.
