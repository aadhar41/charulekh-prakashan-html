About Charulekh Prakashan

We have built an enviable status for ourselves in the spiritual publishing industry since 1990.

Based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we are manufacturers, suppliers, and traders of a diverse range of devotional products. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has earned us the trust of customers across India.

We use simple and easy language in our entire assortment of prayer and mantra books, making spiritual knowledge accessible to everyone. Our incense products are manufactured using premium aromas and the best standards of raw materials.
