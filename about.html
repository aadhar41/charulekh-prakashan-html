Our Sacred Journey

For over three decades, we have been devoted to spreading ancient wisdom and spiritual knowledge, serving as a bridge between timeless traditions and modern seekers on their path to enlightenment.

Our Story

Founded in Faith, Rooted in Tradition

Established in 1990 in the royal city of Jaipur, Charulekh Prakashan began as a humble endeavor to make sacred knowledge accessible to all. Our founder, inspired by the rich spiritual heritage of Rajasthan, envisioned a platform where ancient wisdom could reach modern seekers.

What started as a small collection of astrology books has blossomed into a comprehensive spiritual resource center, serving thousands of devotees across India and beyond. Our journey has been guided by the principle that true knowledge should be shared, not hoarded.

Today, we stand as one of the most trusted names in spiritual literature and devotional products, with a legacy built on authenticity, dedication, and unwavering commitment to our customers' spiritual growth.

Our Journey Through Time

1990

The Beginning

Charulekh Prakashan was founded in Jaipur with a small collection of authentic astrology and spiritual books, driven by the vision to make sacred knowledge accessible to all.

Expanding Horizons

Introduced devotional products including gemstones, incense, and sacred accessories, becoming a one-stop destination for spiritual needs.

2020

Online Education

2025

Digital Platform
