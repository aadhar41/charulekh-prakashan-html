About Charulekh Prakashan

Established in 1990 in Jaipur, Rajasthan, Charulekh Prakashan is a trusted name in devotional publishing and spiritual products. We are dedicated to spreading spiritual wisdom and providing authentic, quality products to seekers across India.

Our Journey

For over three decades, Charulekh Prakashan has been a beacon of spiritual knowledge and devotion. Our journey began with a vision to make spiritual literature and products accessible to everyone, regardless of background or language.

We publish a wide range of books on astrology, numerology, tantra, and devotional practices, all written in simple, easy-to-understand language. Our incense, gemstones, and ritual products are crafted with utmost care and authenticity, ensuring purity and effectiveness.

Our Mission & Values

  • Promote spiritual knowledge and Indian traditions
  • Deliver authentic, quality products and literature
  • Ensure customer satisfaction and trust
  • Support seekers on their spiritual journey
